Wild Oat

More Branding

Project Overview

My purpose was to create an traditional and authentic logo, making it friendly but yet combining personal connection with the client. My plan was to bring the original assets to the new logo. Keeping the same idea but a different look.

Challenge

The Maple Valley logo is very busy; with too many words, little imagery. With it’s bright yellow-orange colouring, at first glance, it could be mistaken for a bargain advertisement.

Concept

I feel the overall look of the new logo is very unique and organic. It doesn’t only connect with them as the client but with their community as well. It brings a warm and homelike atmosphere.

Stuff to use it in

For the wordmark, I made my own hand-lettering. I wanted to keep the home-like idea through the whole concept. The elegance of the curves made it connect well with the logo. The colours were inspired by the fall leaves. Still keeping the connection with the client but also with the community. I also kept the relationship with the teas and the seasons, since their tea becomes more popular during the colder weather.
