Chocolatier - Quotebook

Project Overview

I took a new approach to designing this booklet. I wanted something that did not visually overwhelm you when you looked at it. I wanted something easy to read and visually appealing.

I chose bright fun colours that made the pages pop. I used a sans-serif font to keep the text easy to read and clean combining it with handlettering type making that fun look. I thought of using food and other utilities to express each hand made word.
